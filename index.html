    About Nandu's

    We have been farmers for 50 years and are one
    of the pioneers of the poultry farming industry in
    India. We are not traders buying from other
    sources. We are farmers bringing our products to
    the market, for you!We at Nandu’s believe in a
    way of life that recognizes and honours the
    diversity and interdependence of all life. We
    believe that each one of us makes a difference in
    the world. And we live this belief throughout our
    ecosystem. We care for the planet, for the
    animals, for you!
